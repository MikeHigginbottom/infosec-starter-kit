# Volume Shadow Copy
- Volume Shadow Copy (also known as Shadow Copy, Volume Snapshot Service, Volume Shadow Copy Service or VSS) is a proprietary Microsoft technology, included in their Window operating system, for creating and managing backup copies of files or volumes even when they are in use. [^1] #glossary
