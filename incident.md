# Incident
- Refers to a violation of a company’s security policy [^1] or an adverse network event in an information system or network or the threat of the occurrence of such an event. [^3] An [Event](event.md) transforms into an Incident if the outcome is some kind of unwanted or unplanned effect on the system(s) involved. [^2] #glossary
