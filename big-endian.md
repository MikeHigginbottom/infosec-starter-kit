# big-endian
- A format for storage or transmission of binary data in which the most significant bit (or byte) comes first. The term comes from Gulliver's Travels by Jonathan Swift. The Lilliputians, being very small, had correspondingly small political problems. The Big-Endian and Little-Endian parties debated over whether soft- boiled eggs should be opened at the big end or the little end. See also: little-endian. [^1] #glossary
