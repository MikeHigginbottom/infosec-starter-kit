# Integrated Services Digital Network (ISDN)
- An emerging technology which is beginning to be offered by the telephone carriers of the world. ISDN combines voice and digital network services in a single medium, making it possible to offer customers digital data services as well as voice connections through a single wire. The standards that define ISDN are specified by CCITT. See also: CCITT. [^1] #glossary
