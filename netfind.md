# netfind
- A research prototype to provide a simple Internet white pages user directory. Developed at the University of Colorado, Boulder, it tries to locate telephone and email information given a person's name and a rough description of where the person works. See also: Knowbot, whois, white pages, X.500. [^1] #glossary
