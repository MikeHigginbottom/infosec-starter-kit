# Trans-European Research and Education Networking Association (TERENA)
- TERENA was formed in October 1994 by the merger of RARE and EARN to promote and participate in the development of a high quality international information and telecommunications infrastructure for the benefit of research and education. See also: Reseaux Associes pour la Recherche Europeenne, European Academic and Research Network. [^1] #glossary
