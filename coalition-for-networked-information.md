# Coalition for Networked Information (CNI)
- A consortium formed by American Research Libraries, CAUSE, and EDUCOM (no, they are not acronyms) to promote the creation of, and access to, information resources in networked environments in order to enrich scholarship and enhance intellectual productivity. [^1] #glossary
