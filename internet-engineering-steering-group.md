# Internet Engineering Steering Group (IESG)
- The IESG is composed of the IETF Area Directors and the IETF Chair. It provides the first technical review of Internet standards and is responsible for day-to-day management of the IETF. See also: Internet Engineering Task Force. [^1] #glossary
