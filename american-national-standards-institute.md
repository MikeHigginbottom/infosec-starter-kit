# American National Standards Institute (ANSI)
- This organization is responsible for approving U.S. standards in many areas, including computers and communications. Standards approved by this organization are often called [ansi](notes/infosec/ansi.md) standards (e.g., ANSI C is the version of the C language approved by ANSI). ANSI is a member of ISO. See also: [International Organization for Standardization](iso.md). [^1] #glossary
