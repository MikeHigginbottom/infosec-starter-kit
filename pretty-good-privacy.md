# Pretty Good Privacy (PGP)
- A program, developed by Phil Zimmerman, which cryptographically protects files and electronic mail from being read by others. It may also be used to digitally sign a document or message, thus authenticating the creator. See also: encryption, Data Encryption Standard, RSA. [^1] #glossary
