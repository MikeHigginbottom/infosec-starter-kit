# Federal Networking Council (FNC)
- The coordinating group of representatives from those federal agencies involved in the development and use of federal networking, especially those networks using TCP/IP and the Internet. Current members include representatives from DOD, DOE, ARPA, NSF, NASA, and HHS. See also: Advanced Research Projects Agency, National Science Foundation. [^1] #glossary
