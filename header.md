# Header
- A header is the extra information in a packet that is needed for the protocol stack to process the packet. [^1] #glossary 
- The portion of a packet, preceding the actual data, containing source and destination information. [^2]
- It may also contain error checking and other fields. [^2]
- A header is also the part of an electronic mail message which precedes the body of a message and contains, among other things, the message originator, date and time. [^2]
