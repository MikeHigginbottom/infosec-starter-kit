# Ethics Committee
- A body comprising independent, impartial and multi-disciplinary individuals whose purpose within an organisation is to oversee the implementation and running of the organisation's according to the organisation's own [ethical policies/guidelines](code-of-ethics.md) and/or accepted best practice in the field of ethics. The Ethics Committee may also conduct, oversee or advise on investigations or disciplinary proceedings where ethical concerns are involved. [^1] #glossary
