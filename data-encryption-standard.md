# Data Encryption Standard
- A symmetric-key encryption algorithm created in 1975. DES has several well-publicised vulnerabilities and has been has been withdrawn for use by the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST). The [AES algorithm](advanced-encryption-standard.md) has superseded DES in most cases. [^1] #glossary
