# Remediation
- Activities undertaken by an organisation to limit or stop an attack, often as part of incident response. Remediation includes blocking malicious IP addresses, removing infected files or devices and restoring affected systems to a known good state. [^2] #glossary

- Honest feedback after an incident is important [^1]
