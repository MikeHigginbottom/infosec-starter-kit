# RFCs
- [Request For Comments](request-for-comments.md) documents contain technical specifications and organizational notes for the Internet. [^1] #glossary 

- Only some RFCs are standards. Depending on their maturity level and what they cover, RFCs are labelled with different statuses: Internet Standard, Proposed Standard, Best Current Practice, Experimental, Informational, and Historic. [^1]
