# Biometrics
- A method of [authentication](authentication.md) which relies on, ideally uniquely identifying, biological characteristics of the user being authenticated #glossary 

Examples include: [^1]
- fingerprint
- iris scan
- retina scan
- hand geometry
- signature verification
- voice recognition
- keystroke dynamics
