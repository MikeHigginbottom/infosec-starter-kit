# Wide Area Information Servers (WAIS)
- A distributed information service which offers simple natural language input, indexed searching for fast retrieval, and a relevance feedback mechanism which allows the results of initial searches to influence future searches. Public domain implementations are available. See also: archie, Gopher, Prospero. [^1] #glossary
