# RSA
- A public-key cryptographic system which may be used for encryption and authentication. It was invented in 1977 and named for its inventors: Ron Rivest, Adi Shamir, and Leonard Adleman. See also: encryption, Data Encryption Standard, Pretty Good Privacy. [^1] #glossary
