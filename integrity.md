# Integrity
- One of the three pillars of security: [CIA](confidentiality-integrity-availability.md). The need to ensure that information has not been changed accidentally or deliberately, and that it is accurate and complete. [^1] #glossary

- Integrity is verified through the use of cryptographic [hashing](hashing.md).  [^2] 
- See also [Authenticity](authenticity.md)
